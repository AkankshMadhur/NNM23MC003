Course Abbreviations at NMAMIT

KCET

Karnataka Common Entrance Test
KCET Counselling 2024 Round 1 Option Entry is open for admission to the BE/BTech/BPharm courses. The last date to apply is Jul 30, 2024. Round 1 seat allotment result will be out on Aug 8, 2024.

XAT

Xavier Aptitude Test
The XAT registration 2025 for the MBA/PGDM courses is open now. Candidates can register on the official website. The XAT 2025 exam will be conducted on Jan 5, 2025.

MAT

Management Aptitude Test
The MAT 2024 registration process for the August session is open for IBT Phase 1 and CBT exams. The last date to register is Aug 9, 2024, and Aug 11, 2024, respectively. For the PBT exam, the last date to apply is Aug 18, 2024.

ATMA

AIMS Test for Management Admissions
ATMA 2024 July session registrations are closed. The ATMA 2024 July Session exam will be held on Jul 31, 2024, and the result will be announced on Aug 6, 2024.

Karnataka PGCET

Karnataka Post Graduate Common Entrance Test
The Karnataka PGCET 2024 revised exam date has been announced for admission to MBA/MCA courses, which will be held on Aug 4, 2024.

GATE

Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering
The GATE registration 2025 will commence on Aug 24, 2024, for admission to the MTech course. The GATE 2025 exam will be conducted on Feb 1, 2, 15, and 16, 2025.

KMAT

Karnataka Management Aptitude Test
KMAT Karnataka 2024 registrations are open for admission to MBA/MCA courses. The last date to apply is Aug 31, 2024. The KMAT Karnataka 2024 exam will be conducted in the first week of Nov 2024, tentatively.

CAT

Common Admission Test
The CAT 2024 registration process will commence on Aug 1, 2024, for admission to the MBA course. The CAT 2024 exam will be scheduled on Nov 24, 2024.
